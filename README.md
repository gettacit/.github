# ΤΛCΙΤ

**Your code already has the answers. Now your users do too.**

Tacit turns your codebase into an AI assistant your end users can talk to — no documentation required. It indexes your controllers, services, routes, and business logic, then answers user questions grounded in how your product actually works.

### How it works

1. **Connect your codebase** — local copy or connected remote
2. **Tacit indexes it** — features, rules, and data flow mapped as structured relationships
3. **Users ask questions** — and get accurate, sourced answers about your product

### Built for

- Product companies shipping faster than they can document
- Teams without a dedicated technical writer
- Support teams drowning in "how do I do X" tickets

### Supports

`.NET` · `ASP.NET Core` · `Java` · `Spring Boot` · `Node.js` · `NestJS` · `Express` · `React` · `Angular` · `Vue`

### Deployment

Runs entirely inside your infrastructure. Air-gapped and on-premise deployment supported — no data leaves your network. Built for regulated industries: fintech, healthcare, defense, logistics.
